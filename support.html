Capital Quiz Support

Frequently Asked Questions & Help

Platform: iOS 15.0+ / Android 7.0+ | Languages: 60 | Price: Free (with optional purchases)

Getting Started

How do I play?

Choose a continent or category, then identify the correct capital city for each country shown. Select from four options and try to get the highest score possible!

What regions are available?

You can explore capitals from 7 regions:

  • Europe
  • Asia
  • Africa
  • North America
  • South America
  • Oceania
  • World (all capitals combined)

How many capitals are there?

The app includes over 200 capital cities from countries and territories around the world.

Game Modes

What game modes are available?

  • Standard - Learn at your own pace with no pressure
  • Timed - Race against the clock
  • Lives - Limited attempts, maximum challenge
  • Survival - How long can you last?
  • Endless - Keep playing until you decide to stop

What is the Daily Challenge?

Every day there's a new quiz challenge available. Complete it to build your streak and earn special rewards. The challenge resets at midnight in your local time zone.

Streaks & Achievements

How do streaks work?

Play at least one quiz every day to build your streak. Your streak increases each consecutive day you play. If you miss a day, your streak resets to zero.

How many achievements are there?

There are 75+ achievements to unlock across various categories including beginner, progress, mastery, speed, streak, challenge, dedication, and skill achievements.

Do achievements sync with Game Center?

Yes! If you sign in with Game Center (iOS) or Google Play Games (Android), your achievements will sync and be visible in your gaming profile.

Game Center & Cloud Sync

How do I connect to Game Center?

Go to Settings in the app and tap on "Sign in with Game Center" (iOS) or "Sign in with Play Games" (Android). Once connected, your achievements and leaderboard scores will sync.

Can I compete with friends?

Yes! Connect to Game Center or Play Games to see global leaderboards and compare your scores with friends and players worldwide.

Is my progress synced across devices?

When signed in to Game Center or Play Games, your achievements and leaderboard scores sync across devices. Local statistics are stored on each device.

Languages

How do I change the language?

The app automatically uses your device's language setting. To change:

  • iOS: Settings > Capital Quiz > Language
  • Android: Settings > Apps > Capital Quiz > Language

What languages are supported?

The app supports 60 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Arabic, Hindi, Portuguese, Russian, Ukrainian, and many more.

Purchases & Ads

Is the app free?

Yes! The app is free to download and play. It includes ads which help support development.

How do I remove ads?

You can purchase the premium version from within the app to remove all advertisements and enjoy an ad-free experience.

How do I restore purchases?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any previous purchases made with your Apple ID or Google account.

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing. What should I do?

  • Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store or Play Store
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Restart your device
  • If problems persist, contact us

My progress was lost. Can I recover it?

If you were signed in to Game Center or Play Games, your achievements should be recoverable. Local statistics are stored on your device and may not be recoverable if the app was uninstalled.

How do I reset my progress?

Go to Settings > Advanced > Reset to Defaults. Note that this will clear all local statistics. Game Center/Play Games achievements cannot be reset.
